# GPT Test Scenarios

Purpose: use these scenarios to test a future Creator Brand Matching GPT before trusting it with real manager workflows.

These are not recommendations. They are test cases. A good GPT should use the Knowledge files to rank opportunities, explain pitch angles, and avoid bad-fit deals.

## Scenario 1: UK Lifestyle Creator

Creator profile: UK-based female lifestyle creator on TikTok and Instagram. Content covers daily routines, home, soft beauty, fashion, and casual product reviews. Audience is mostly UK women aged 18 to 34. Minimum fixed fee is 1000 USD.

Expected strongest pitch angles: UK Creator, Lifestyle Creator, Beauty Creator, Fashion Creator, Fixed Fee Preferred.

Expected top opportunity types: paid campaign, UGC, app promotion, whitelisting if usage is paid.

Expected top agency types: UK or global creator marketing agency, PR agency with lifestyle brands, talent platform with fixed-fee briefs.

Opportunities that should be avoided: affiliate-only offers, US-only briefs, low-budget PR gifting, music campaigns with no lifestyle angle.

Why: geography and lifestyle niche should matter more than platform alone. Fixed fee should be protected because the creator has a clear fee floor.

## Scenario 2: US Parenting Creator

Creator profile: US-based mom creator on TikTok and Instagram. Content covers parenting, family routines, toddler products, home gadgets, and mom lifestyle. Audience is mostly US parents and young families. Minimum fee is 1500 USD.

Expected strongest pitch angles: US Creator, Parenting Creator, Family Audience, Lifestyle Creator, Fixed Fee Preferred.

Expected top opportunity types: paid campaign, app promotion, home product campaign, parenting product campaign, UGC with paid usage.

Expected top agency types: family/lifestyle creator agency, brand direct, PR agency with parenting or home accounts.

Opportunities that should be avoided: affiliate-only, adult or nightlife brands, low-budget song promotion, male sports-only campaigns.

Why: parenting and geography are the strongest matching signals. The GPT should not recommend generic TikTok opportunities unless they fit parenting or family use.
